Privacy Statement

Information about you: what we collect

We collect following personal information:

  • Contact information, such as your name and email address
  • Department and institution affiliations
  • ORCID identifier
  • IP address

Information about you: how we use it and with whom we share it

The information you provide will be used by us to provide access to the service and to record data provenance.

We are using information about you because it is necessary for the core functions of the repository, such as giving credit and attribution to the datasets, monitoring access to the data and operations on the data. In legal terms we are using your personal data on the basis of legitimate interest.

We will hold the personal data you provided to us for as long as the service is live

Information about you will not be shared with third parties.

If you have any questions or requests, please contact biodare (@) ed.ac.uk

Online Tracking

We may use third-party web analytics services on our Site, such as Google Analytics. In doing so information about your use of our website, including your IP address, may be transmitted to Google and stored on servers in the United States. This information creates an overall picture of website use, and is never identified individually or personally and is not linked to personal information that we store about you.
